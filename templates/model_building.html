📊 Understanding the Metrics

  • Accuracy: Overall correctness of predictions
  • Precision: How many predicted frauds are actually frauds
  • Recall: How many actual frauds were detected
  • F1-Score: Balance between precision and recall (best overall metric)

The model with the highest F1-Score was selected for fraud detection.
